ABOUT

THEMES: COMING SOON

The 6th International Conference on Technology and Innovation Management 2026 (ICTIM 2026) is where innovation and sustainability converge to shape a brighter future. This prestigious event will be hosted by the Faculty of Business (FOB) at Multimedia University. This conference stands as a premier gathering, uniting a diverse audience of academic researchers, industry leaders, and policymakers. Together, we will delve into the profound implications of our digital era, exploring the latest trends and advancements in technology, innovation management, and business, all through the lens of sustainability. At ICTIM 2026, the core objectives are to facilitate dynamic knowledge exchange, foster valuable networking opportunities, provide insights into policy considerations, and examine the profound societal impact of technology and innovation management. This conference invites you to be part of a vibrant community of forward-thinkers, eager to explore the frontiers of innovation, and to actively shape a sustainable tomorrow.

KEYNOTE SPEAKERS

Associate Professor Dr Abdul Rahim Ridzuan

Professor
Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM)

Assoc. Prof. Dr. Abdul Rahim Ridzuan is a Senior Fellow at the Institute of Big Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (IBDAAI), Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Malaysia. With 16 years of academic service, he specializes in international, energy, and environmental economics, with strong expertise in time series modelling and sustainable development. Dr. Abdul Rahim has published over 230 scholarly works, including 45 in Web of Science and 93 in Scopus, and has secured more than RM4.4 million (total 55 grants) in national and international research grants. He has served extensively as a journal editor, reviewer, consultant, keynote speaker, and external assessor worldwide. Recognized globally, he holds multiple visiting and adjunct professorships across Malaysia, Indonesia, Kazakhstan, Nigeria, and beyond. His contributions to research and policy development have earned him numerous awards, including the Scopus Research Star Award 2025 by MOHE and Elsevier.
