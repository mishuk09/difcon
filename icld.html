5 - 7 MAY 2026, Cyberjaya Malaysia

ABOUT

THEMES: COMING SOON

The Faculty of Law, Multimedia University will hold the 6th International Conference on Law and Digitalization 2026 (ICLD 2026) on 5-7 May 2026 (Hybrid Conference). ICLD2026 will be part of the bigger Digital Future Congress (DIFCON2026). ICLD2026 aims to provide a platform for both local and international academics, practitioners, policymakers, researchers, and students to meet, share ideas and knowledge in law and contemporary legal issues through paper presentations. It also encourages academic linkages between the academicians and the researchers from all areas of law and other related disciplines. Thus, ICLD 2026 promotes future co-operation among intellectuals on interdisciplinary law and society.

PUBLICATIONS

All accepted and presented papers will be published in collaborating journals according to technical program committee and reviewers’ recommendations.

  • 1.Malaysian Journal of Syariah and Law (SCOPUS/ MyCite)
  • 2.Global Journal Al-Thaqafah (SCOPUS/ MyCite)
  • 3.Asian Journal of Law and Governance (MyCite)
