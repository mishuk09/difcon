5 - 7 MAY 2026, Cyberjaya Malaysia

ABOUT

THEMES: Sustainable Creativity in the Age of AI and Multimedia Technology

The sixth edition of International Conference on Creative Multimedia (ICCM 2026) will take place from May 5-7, 2026, in conjunction with the Digital Futures International Congress 2026 (DIFCON 2026). DIFCON 2026 is a premier event featuring six co-located conferences. ICCM 2026 is mainly organised by the Faculty of Creative Multimedia (FCM) and the Faculty of Cinematic Arts (FCA) at Multimedia University, Malaysia with the partnership from various co-organisers and collaborators. Aligned with the 2030 SDGs, the congress and conferences will be conducted in hybrid mode, offering participants the flexibility to join either on-site or virtually. The conference this year centres on sustainability and conscientiousness as key themes for discussion, specifically addressing global environmental, social and governance issues. It will serve as a gathering place for researchers and practitioners across creative arts, design, Information and Communication Technology (ICT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and other related industries. The primary aim is to amplify the voice of sustainable creativity through the integration of AI and creative multimedia technology. Authors are strongly encouraged to feature their creative concepts, prototypes, proofs-of-concept, and detailed innovation case studies relevant to the conference themes or tracks. This event is targeted at researchers, academicians, industry participants, undergraduates, and postgraduate students seeking fresh insights and new research ideas within the creative and multimedia industries. Keywords of the conference are Creativity, Media Art and Design, AI and Generative AI, Multimedia Technology, Sustainability, Creative Sustainability, Creative Industries

PUBLICATION

    All tracks are grounded in creative sustainability and conscientious practices within the technologically advanced era. High-quality articles will be invited for extension and consideration for the book proposal "Sustainable Creativity in the Age of AI and Multimedia Technology" which is under evaluation for inclusion in the IEREK Interdisciplinary Book Series, published by Springer Nature (Advances in Science, Technology & Innovation (ASTI), https://link.springer.com/series/15883, Scopus Indexed). Invited authors must ensure the papers similarity rate should not exceed 10-15% per chapter and no more than 5% from any single source and any form of plagiarism or detected AI-generated content may result in the rejection of the articles from the final book submission. Other accepted papers will be invited for MYCITE journals or others, subject to meeting the individual journal's requirements.

  • Malaysian Journal of Learning and Instruction (MJLI) (SCOPUS Q1)

  • The International Journal of Creative Multimedia (IJCM), MMU Press Journal (MYCITE and MyJurnal)

  • Advanced Research in Applied Sciences and Engineering Technology (Scopus Q2)

  • Alam Cipta: International Journal on Sustainable Tropical Design Research and Practice (Scopus Q4)

    • Template Guideline


    All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference. A minimum of 5 and a maximum of 10 page(s) manuscript is to be prepared according to the following template.

CONFERENCE TRACKS

  • AI, Generative AI, and Multimedia Technology for Sustainability

    AI Generated Contents and Techniques, Generative Music and Composition, Generative Assets, AI-driven Tools for Creative Contents, Application of Generative Models, Human-AI Co-Creation, Computer Graphics and Techniques, Animation and Visual Effects Techniques, Digital Audio, Sound Effects and Techniques, Interactive Multimedia (AR, VR, XR, Metaverse), 2D / 3D Game Asset, Game Development, Vibe Coding.

  • Creativity, Art, and Design with Conscientiousness

    Sustainable Creativity, User-Centred Design, Design for Social Change, User Experience, Interface Design, Brand Experience, Gendered Design, Interactive Design, Affective Design, Ergonomic Study, System Usability, Creative Coding, Generative Art, Interactive Installation, Experimental Media, Drone Technology, Projection Arts, Digital Creativity, Computational Creativity, Interactive Electronics, Information Design, Mobile Design and Application, Advertising Design, New Product Development, Design Workflow.

  • Creative Talents: Learning and Development

    AI in Education, Creative Pedagogy for Sustainability, Education Technology, Learning Analytic, Intelligent Tutoring, Personalised Learning, Smart Learning Environment, Mobile Learning, Future of Education, Talent Workforce, Art & Design Education, Creative Talents for Sustainability, Quality in Higher Education; STEM Education, Training and Development.

  • Sustainable Culture, Space and Heritage Preservation

    Sustainable Design, Design Collaboration, Environmental Design, Metaverse Design and Virtual Environment, Culture and Heritage Design, Architectural Design, Design for Smart City, Green Design, Design for Health, AI-Human Co-design.

  • Storytelling, Film & Animation, Media and the Creative Industries

    Digital Cinematic, Motion Capture, Streaming Media, Virtual Production Process, Interactive Movie, Drone Creative Videography, Media Culture and Technology, Media and Communication, Social Media, Creative Industry, Digital Content, Digital Marketing, Future of Creative Work.

  • Responsible AI and AI Governance in Creative Spaces

    Ethical Co-Creation and Human-AI Collaboration, Algorithmic Bias in Visual and Media Representation, IP and Authorship, Sustainable AI for Environment, AI Governance for Vulnerable Users, Deception and Deepfakes

  • Creativity and Sustainability for Future Innovation and Application

    Circular Design and Lifecycle Thinking, Nature and Indigenous Creative Innovation, Low-Carbon Media, Sustainable Behavioural Change, Education for Sustainable Creativity, Localized Design and Production
