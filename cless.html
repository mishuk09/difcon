ABOUT

THEMES: COMING SOON

The 7th International Conference on Communication, Language, Education and Social Sciences (CLESS 2026) will be held on 5-7 May 2026. CLESS 2026 is an integral part of the larger Digital Future Congress (DIFCON), encompassing various conferences in diverse fields. DIFCON-CLESS 2026 brings together scholars, educators, researchers, and professionals to explore how digitalization reshapes human interaction, knowledge dissemination, societal structures, and linguistic evolution. This conference serves as a collaborative space for participants to engage in meaningful discussions, foster collaboration, exchange knowledge, and discuss strategies to foster inclusive, ethical, and sustainable digital societies.
